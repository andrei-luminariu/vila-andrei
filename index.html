The guesthouse

Vila Andrei, which was renovated at the beginning of 2020, offers 12 accommodation spaces organized in 6 rooms, all with the same facilities: LED TV, free Wi-Fi, towels, hairdryer.

The guesthouse is located about 200m away from the city centre, on the left side, next to Hotel Olănești. In just 5 minutes you can reach the central park where the indoor pools with sulfurous water are or the outdoor pool, located at 75m from us. It is also very easy to reach the local market as well as the main spa of the city.

Facilities

On the 1st floor, there are 5 double en-suite bedrooms with balcony. On this floor you can also find the kitchen, which is fully-fitted and can accommodate around 8-10 people at a time.

In the attic, you can find another en-suite room with all the necessary facilities, but without a balcony. Beside this, the living room of about 30sqm offers you a bright and quiet space for relaxation. We also offer various board games (Puzzle, Rummy, Backgammon, Chess, Monopoly, Catan).

The guesthouse's yard is large and also includes parking for 5-6 cars. You can also spend time on the patio, which features a fully-fitted barbeque.
